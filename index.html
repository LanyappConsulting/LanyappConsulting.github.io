Professional Drafting and Design Services

We're a small company, but don't let that fool you: we have decades of experience in industrial design, and drafting and CAD services. Below are some of our more popular and famous services. For more information, please see our Portfolio, or feel free to Contact Us with a question!

Refractories and Ceramics Design

We understand the importance of the refractory lining, and of assessing the internal vessel atmospheric conditions which it must be designed to interact with. Lanyapp designs both brick and castable linings, customized for your job needs. We offer in-house calculations for ring and dome combinations, so that your field workers have the right brick to do the right job. We can guide you on what type of refractory material is best-suited for your job, to ensure that your lining's integrity will not be compromised.

  • Kilns (Rotary and Tunnel)
  • Sulfur Recovery Units (SRUs)
  • Combustion Chambers
  • Induction Furnaces

2D and 3D Drafting and Design

While our design specialty is in refractories, we also offer high-quality drafting services for other fields, including structural, mechanical, and general schematics. We create clean, comprehensible drawings, as well as 3D models. We take pride in our work, believing that technical drawings should convey a clear story on what is to be built. Our emphasis is on clarity, and we utilize artistic skill to reduce drawing clutter and aid in readability, giving you a document that will impress at first sight.

  • 2D AutoCAD
  • 3D modeling with Autodesk Inventor
  • CAD/3D Training
  • Finite Element Analysis

About Lanyapp Consulting

Lanyapp Consulting, LLC, was founded in Coraopolis, PA (near Pittsburgh) in early 2014.

  • Why the Name?

    Our name "Lanyapp" is derived from the Louisiana-French word, "Lagniappe," (pronounced "lan-yap") which roughly means "to give extra from what is expected." This summarizes our work ethic -- we don't just give you what you expect; we go above and beyond to ensure that the services we deliver make the most professional and positive impact possible.

  • The Lanyapp Vision

    Lanyapp was founded to be an awesome science, technology and engineering company. Aside from providing high-quality work and products, we invest in research into the latest technologies that may help our clients, such as 3D Printing and the Internet of Things. After all, who said work had to be boring and no fun?

  • Company Principles

    Aside from our technical expertise, we pride ourselves on our principles. We give accurate and fair quotes, work closely with our clients to deliver on schedule, and tell it like it is, even if it hurts our own bottom line -- because we believe quality and trust are our most important assets.

  • The Future

    Lanyapp's vision and principles are guiding us to a brighter future. We're making plans for expansion and we're excited to roll out new services and products for our clients in the near future. We look forward to working with you!

Kiln Concept

A 3D kiln concept using Autodesk Inventor.

This is an example concept for a kiln made for a customer (proprietary details removed of course). The concept was created as a 3D model in Autodesk Inventor, which allowed easy discussion and visualization with the customer. When the details were agreed upon, the 3D model was used to generate 2D CAD drawings to actually be printed for the field work. Using a 3D model first improved clarity and allowed quicker turn-around time on changes than the traditional all-in-2D-CAD model.
